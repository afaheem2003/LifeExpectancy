Life Expectancies at Birth in MONMOUTH

*Please note that there may be missing data for Black Americans due to inequalities in data reporting.

Analysis

From 1987 to 2009, average male life expectancy in MONMOUTH increased by approximately 6 years. The United States' average male life expectancy increased by 5 years over the same time period. From 1987 to 2009, average female life expectancy in MONMOUTH increased by approximately 4 years. The United States' average female life expectancy increased by 3 years over the same time period. From 1987 to 2009 the average male life expectancy in MONMOUTH was greater than the United States' average male life expectancy. From 1987 to 1993 the average female life expectancy in MONMOUTH was less than the United States' average female life expectancy. In 1994 the average female life expectancy in MONMOUTH was greater than the United States' average female life expectancy. In 1995 the average female life expectancy in MONMOUTH was equal to the United States' average female life expectancy. From 1996 to 1997 the average female life expectancy in MONMOUTH was greater than the United States' average female life expectancy. In 1998 the average female life expectancy in MONMOUTH was equal to the United States' average female life expectancy. From 1999 to 2009 the average female life expectancy in MONMOUTH was greater than the United States' average female life expectancy.

All data from: The Global Health Data Exchange
