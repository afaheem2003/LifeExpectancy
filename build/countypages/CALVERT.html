Life Expectancies at Birth in CALVERT

*Please note that there may be missing data for Black Americans due to inequalities in data reporting.

Analysis

From 1987 to 2009, average male life expectancy in CALVERT increased by approximately 4 years. The United States' average male life expectancy increased by 5 years over the same time period. From 1987 to 2009, average female life expectancy in CALVERT increased by approximately 2 years. The United States' average female life expectancy increased by 3 years over the same time period. From 1987 to 2004 the average male life expectancy in CALVERT was greater than the United States' average male life expectancy. From 2005 to 2006 the average male life expectancy in CALVERT was equal to the United States' average male life expectancy. From 2007 to 2009 the average male life expectancy in CALVERT was less than the United States' average male life expectancy. From 1987 to 1991 the average female life expectancy in CALVERT was less than the United States' average female life expectancy. In 1992 the average female life expectancy in CALVERT was equal to the United States' average female life expectancy. In 1993 the average female life expectancy in CALVERT was less than the United States' average female life expectancy. From 1994 to 2003 the average female life expectancy in CALVERT was greater than the United States' average female life expectancy. In 2004 the average female life expectancy in CALVERT was equal to the United States' average female life expectancy. From 2005 to 2009 the average female life expectancy in CALVERT was less than the United States' average female life expectancy.

All data from: The Global Health Data Exchange
