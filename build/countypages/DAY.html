Analysis

From 1987 to 2009, average male life expectancy in DAY increased by approximately 4 years. The United States' average male life expectancy increased by 5 years over the same time period. From 1987 to 2009, average female life expectancy in DAY increased by approximately 2 years. The United States' average female life expectancy increased by 3 years over the same time period. From 1987 to 1998 the average male life expectancy in DAY was greater than the United States' average male life expectancy. In 1999 the average male life expectancy in DAY was less than the United States' average male life expectancy. From 2000 to 2002 the average male life expectancy in DAY was greater than the United States' average male life expectancy. In 2003 the average male life expectancy in DAY was equal to the United States' average male life expectancy. From 2004 to 2008 the average male life expectancy in DAY was less than the United States' average male life expectancy. In 2009 the average male life expectancy in DAY was greater than the United States' average male life expectancy. From 1987 to 2009 the average female life expectancy in DAY was greater than the United States' average female life expectancy.

All data from: The Global Health Data Exchange
