Life Expectancies at Birth in JUNEAU

*Please note that there may be missing data for Black Americans due to inequalities in data reporting.

Analysis

From 1987 to 2009, average male life expectancy in JUNEAU increased by approximately 4 years. The United States' average male life expectancy increased by 5 years over the same time period. From 1987 to 2009, average female life expectancy in JUNEAU increased by approximately 2 years. The United States' average female life expectancy increased by 3 years over the same time period. From 1987 to 1997 the average male life expectancy in JUNEAU was greater than the United States' average male life expectancy. In 1998 the average male life expectancy in JUNEAU was less than the United States' average male life expectancy. From 1999 to 2008 the average male life expectancy in JUNEAU was greater than the United States' average male life expectancy. In 2009 the average male life expectancy in JUNEAU was less than the United States' average male life expectancy. From 1987 to 2006 the average female life expectancy in JUNEAU was greater than the United States' average female life expectancy. In 2007 the average female life expectancy in JUNEAU was equal to the United States' average female life expectancy. From 2008 to 2009 the average female life expectancy in JUNEAU was less than the United States' average female life expectancy.

All data from: The Global Health Data Exchange
