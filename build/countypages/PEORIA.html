Life Expectancies at Birth in PEORIA

*Please note that there may be missing data for Black Americans due to inequalities in data reporting.

Analysis

From 1987 to 2009, average male life expectancy in PEORIA increased by approximately 3 years. The United States' average male life expectancy increased by 5 years over the same time period. From 1987 to 2009, average female life expectancy in PEORIA increased by approximately 1 years. The United States' average female life expectancy increased by 3 years over the same time period. From 1987 to 1992 the average male life expectancy in PEORIA was greater than the United States' average male life expectancy. In 1993 the average male life expectancy in PEORIA was equal to the United States' average male life expectancy. In 1994 the average male life expectancy in PEORIA was greater than the United States' average male life expectancy. In 1995 the average male life expectancy in PEORIA was equal to the United States' average male life expectancy. In 1996 the average male life expectancy in PEORIA was greater than the United States' average male life expectancy. From 1997 to 2000 the average male life expectancy in PEORIA was less than the United States' average male life expectancy. In 2001 the average male life expectancy in PEORIA was equal to the United States' average male life expectancy. From 2002 to 2009 the average male life expectancy in PEORIA was less than the United States' average male life expectancy. From 1987 to 2004 the average female life expectancy in PEORIA was greater than the United States' average female life expectancy. From 2005 to 2009 the average female life expectancy in PEORIA was less than the United States' average female life expectancy.

All data from: The Global Health Data Exchange
