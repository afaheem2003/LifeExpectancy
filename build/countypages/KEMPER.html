Life Expectancies at Birth in KEMPER

*Please note that there may be missing data for Black Americans due to inequalities in data reporting.

Analysis

From 1987 to 2009, average male life expectancy in KEMPER increased by approximately 3 years. The United States' average male life expectancy increased by 5 years over the same time period. From 1987 to 2009, average female life expectancy in KEMPER increased by approximately 1 years. The United States' average female life expectancy increased by 3 years over the same time period. From 1987 to 2009 the average male life expectancy in KEMPER was less than the United States' average male life expectancy. From 1987 to 2009 the average female life expectancy in KEMPER was less than the United States' average female life expectancy.

All data from: The Global Health Data Exchange
