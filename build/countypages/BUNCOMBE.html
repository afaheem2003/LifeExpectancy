Life Expectancies at Birth in BUNCOMBE

*Please note that there may be missing data for Black Americans due to inequalities in data reporting.

Analysis

From 1987 to 2009, average male life expectancy in BUNCOMBE increased by approximately 4 years. The United States' average male life expectancy increased by 5 years over the same time period. From 1987 to 2009, average female life expectancy in BUNCOMBE increased by approximately 2 years. The United States' average female life expectancy increased by 3 years over the same time period. From 1987 to 1991 the average male life expectancy in BUNCOMBE was greater than the United States' average male life expectancy. From 1992 to 1993 the average male life expectancy in BUNCOMBE was less than the United States' average male life expectancy. In 1994 the average male life expectancy in BUNCOMBE was greater than the United States' average male life expectancy. In 1995 the average male life expectancy in BUNCOMBE was equal to the United States' average male life expectancy. In 1996 the average male life expectancy in BUNCOMBE was greater than the United States' average male life expectancy. From 1997 to 2009 the average male life expectancy in BUNCOMBE was less than the United States' average male life expectancy. From 1987 to 1989 the average female life expectancy in BUNCOMBE was greater than the United States' average female life expectancy. In 1990 the average female life expectancy in BUNCOMBE was equal to the United States' average female life expectancy. From 1991 to 2001 the average female life expectancy in BUNCOMBE was greater than the United States' average female life expectancy. In 2002 the average female life expectancy in BUNCOMBE was equal to the United States' average female life expectancy. From 2003 to 2005 the average female life expectancy in BUNCOMBE was less than the United States' average female life expectancy. From 2006 to 2007 the average female life expectancy in BUNCOMBE was greater than the United States' average female life expectancy. From 2008 to 2009 the average female life expectancy in BUNCOMBE was less than the United States' average female life expectancy.

All data from: The Global Health Data Exchange
